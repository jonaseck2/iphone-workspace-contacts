## Get colleague caller ID on your iPhone

Hi! We built a small internal app, **WorkspaceContacts**, that puts the Imeto team directory into
your iPhone Contacts — so when a colleague calls, you see their **name** instead of a number, and
you can find them by name in the Phone app.

### Install (2 minutes)

1. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store (Apple's app for trying internal apps).
2. Open the invite link we sent you and tap **Accept**, then **Install**.
3. Open **WorkspaceContacts**.

### Set it up

4. Tap **Sign in with Google** and use your **@imeto.com** account.
5. You'll see the colleague list. Tap **Enable & sync** and, when iOS asks, **Allow** Contacts
   access.
6. Done. Your colleagues are now in Contacts and caller ID will show their names.

### Good to know

- **Where the contacts live:** they go into your normal iPhone Contacts, in an "Imeto Directory"
  group. If you use iCloud Contacts, they may sync to your other Apple devices — that's expected.
- **Removing them:** anytime, in the app: **⋯ (top-right) → "Remove all synced contacts."**
  Signing out also removes them.
- **Staying current:** the app refreshes in the background, so new colleagues and number changes
  show up automatically.
- **Only Imeto:** sign-in is limited to `@imeto.com` accounts, and the app only reads our own
  Workspace directory.
